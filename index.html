What is Connected Weekend ?

Connected weekend is an event of two days, organized by CSE, the event will hold a conference that introduces the participants to the world of IoT (internet of things), addition to that ,two workshops are planed , the first one will be about building a basic project and the second one will cover more topics . The event will be wrapped up by a competition between teams where the winner will have an award

Conference

The event will start with a conference ,and during this conference , we will be disccussing an overview of the Io with experts speakers . There will also be a simple concept about the needs and requirements of IoT projects: talking about Arduino, Raspberry Pi
